Unicorns

The unicorn is a legendary creature that has been described since antiquity as a beast with a single large,
pointed, spiraling horn projecting from its forehead.
In European literature and art, the unicorn has for the last thousand years or
so been depicted as a white horse-like or goat-like animal with a long straight horn with spiralling grooves,
cloven hooves, and sometimes a goat's beard.
